Meet the Team

The People Behind the Mission

A Team Built Around Grief-Informed Care

At The Grief Center, our team is made up of licensed therapists, clinical supervisors, and support staff who share a deep commitment to normalizing grief and providing compassionate, expert care. Each member brings unique strengths, lived experience, and specialized training to the work we do.

We believe the quality of care begins with the quality of the people providing it. That's why every member of our team is trained in grief-informed, trauma-responsive approaches — ensuring that no matter who you work with, you'll receive care that honors your experience.

Christal R. Pennie

LPC-MHSP
Founder & Chief Grief Executive

Christal is the visionary behind The Grief Center — Nashville's grief ecosystem for therapy, education, supervision, and community care. With deep expertise in grief counseling, clinical supervision, and trauma-informed practice, she leads a team dedicated to changing how our society understands and responds to loss.

Through direct client care, professional training, and The GRip Spot Podcast, Christal is building a movement to normalize grief.

Jeremy McKinnon
EMDR TRAINED
Therapist — Individuals & Couples

Reaching out for support takes courage. Whether you're navigating grief, relationship challenges, trauma, or life transitions, you deserve a space that feels safe, steady, and grounded. Jeremy works with individuals and couples, offering compassionate, evidence-based care with night and weekend availability to meet the needs of busy professionals, caregivers, and families. His approach is collaborative, respectful, and rooted in the belief that healing happens when people feel understood—not judged or rushed.
