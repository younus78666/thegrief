Community Support

Grief Groups — You Are Not Alone

Find strength in shared experience. Our facilitated grief support groups offer a safe, compassionate space to process loss alongside others who understand.

What We Offer

Guided Grief Support in Community

Grief support groups provide a collective space for individuals experiencing loss to come together, share their stories, and find strength in community. Guided by experienced facilitators, these groups foster belonging and mutual understanding — reminding you that you are not walking this path alone.

This is not group therapy. Grief support groups are a guided, peer-centered experience rooted in shared understanding, emotional safety, and practical coping. Each session is designed to honor your story while connecting you with others navigating similar experiences.

Why Join a Group

The Power of Shared Experience

Shared Understanding

Hearing others' stories can provide insight into your own grief journey. Participants relate to one another's experiences, creating a deep sense of belonging that is difficult to find elsewhere.

Emotional Safety

Our groups create a judgment-free environment where every emotion is welcome. Experienced facilitators guide each session, ensuring all voices are heard, validated, and respected.

Coping & Growth

Group members share coping strategies and approaches that have worked for them. Learning from others' journeys can inspire new ways of navigating grief and finding meaning after loss.

Family Grief Sessions
