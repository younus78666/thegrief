Education & Consulting

Training & Consultation

Equipping professionals, organizations, and communities with grief-informed knowledge, tools, and strategies.

Beyond the Therapy Room

Grief Education for Every Setting

The Grief Center's impact extends far beyond individual therapy. Through professional training, organizational consultation, and community education, we equip the people and systems around grief with the knowledge they need to respond with compassion, competence, and care.

Whether you're a healthcare organization looking to build grief-informed practices, a school navigating student loss, a corporate team supporting grieving employees, or a community group seeking education on grief — our training and consultation services are designed to meet you where you are.

Our Training Programs

Professional & Community Training

Grief-Informed Workplace Training

Help your team understand grief, support grieving colleagues, and create a workplace culture that acknowledges loss. Our workplace trainings are designed for HR professionals, managers, and teams who want to respond to grief with empathy and effectiveness.

  • Recognizing grief in the workplace
  • Supporting bereaved employees
  • Creating grief-informed policies
  • Manager and HR training

Community & Faith-Based Education

Grief doesn't only show up in therapy offices. Our community education programs bring grief-informed knowledge to faith communities, schools, nonprofits, and civic organizations — empowering everyday people to support those around them who are grieving.

  • Grief literacy for communities
  • Supporting grieving families
  • Grief in schools and youth
  • Faith-informed grief care

Clinical & Professional Development

For mental health professionals seeking to deepen their grief-informed practice. Our professional development programs include workshops, seminars, and intensive trainings on grief therapy techniques, ethical considerations, and emerging research.

  • Advanced grief therapy techniques
  • Ethics in bereavement care
  • Cultural competency in grief
  • Research-informed practice

Consultation

Organizational & Clinical Consultation

The Grief Center offers consultation services for organizations and professionals who want to build grief-informed systems, programs, and practices. Whether you're developing a bereavement support program, creating grief policies, or seeking clinical guidance for complex cases — our team brings deep expertise to the table.

Our consultation is collaborative, practical, and tailored to your specific needs. We work alongside you to develop solutions that are sustainable, culturally responsive, and grounded in evidence-based practice.

What Consultation Includes

  • Program development & design
  • Grief policy consultation
  • Clinical case consultation
  • Organizational grief assessments
  • Staff training & development
  • Curriculum development for grief education
