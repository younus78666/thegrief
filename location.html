Your First Visit

What to Expect When You Arrive

A Restorative Space

Our office is designed to feel warm, safe, and calming. From the moment you walk in, you'll know you're in a space built for healing.

Confidential & Private

Your privacy is our priority. Our scheduling, waiting areas, and session rooms are designed to protect your confidentiality at every step.

Welcoming & Accessible

We welcome people of all backgrounds, identities, and experiences. Our space and our team are committed to creating an inclusive, affirming environment.

Directions

Getting to The Grief Center

The Grief Center is located at 2603 Elm Hill Pike, Suite K, in Nashville, Tennessee — conveniently situated near the Nashville International Airport and easily accessible from I-40 and I-24.

  • From Downtown Nashville

    Take I-40 East to Exit 215 (Elm Hill Pike). Turn right onto Elm Hill Pike. Our office is on the right.

  • From the Airport

    Head west on Elm Hill Pike. Our office is approximately 5 minutes from Nashville International Airport.

  • Parking

    Free on-site parking is available directly in front of our building.
